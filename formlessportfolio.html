Formless, Berkin Gülten

2019- … Anadolu University, Institute of Fine Arts, Department of Sculpture, Proficiency in Arts, Eskisehir

Biography: Berkin Gülten was born in Çanakkale in 1994. He completed his master’s degree by studying G. Bataille. He is an artist whose works are based on formlessness and eroticism. Currently, he is continuing his doctorate education in Anadolu University Department of Sculpture on the limits of form.
